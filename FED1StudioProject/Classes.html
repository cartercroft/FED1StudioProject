Class Subjects

Music Theory

This class teaches students all about music theory. How to read music, how to write music, it also teaches students about musical concepts such as keys, scales, modes, and other topics.

Guitar Techniques

This class teaches students techniques they can use while playing the guitar. It starts off with the basics such as teaching students how to hold their pick, but also teaches more in-depth techniques such as tremolo picking, string skipping, sweep picking, and tapping.

Guitar Maintence

Learn how to properly take care of your guitar. This course includes classes on changing strings, installing new pickups, and maintaining your guitar to ensure that it has the longest lifespan possible.

Band Management

This class teaches students how to maintain a band. It teaches students how to be leaders, how to work with other musicians, and how to effectively be the frontman for their band.

Audio Editing 101

This class teaches students all there is to know about editing their soundtracks. The editor we use is called Audacity and it is very straightforward and easy to use.

Vocals 101

This class is intended for guitarists who also want to sing. Students will learn how to extend their vocal range with a variety of exercises and techniques. This class covers vocal warmups, exercises, and techniques.
