About Us

We are Guitar Galaxy, we provide musical education for all those wanting to learn a new instrument, or those who want to broaden their musical knowledge. We are a team of musicians who love to teach all we have to share. We believe that everyone has the potential to become a musical virtuoso, they just need the guidance of someone with experience.

Why Choose Guitar Galaxy?

Our Instructors Have Experience

At Guitar Galaxy, our instructors are very well versed in the art of guitar and are eager to teach their students all there is to know about technique and music theory. We believe that it is important for every aspiring musician to have some basic understanding of music theory in order to reach their full potential. Our goal is to educate as many aspiring guitarists as we can with efficiency and effectiveness.

We ALL Have To Start Somewhere!

Listen... We have all been at the beginning stages of learning an instrument and not knowing where to begin. That's where we come into play! Our instructors are happy to work with guitarists of any skill level. If you are just a beginner, our instructors are happy to help give you some tips on starting your journey to guitar mastery! While learning guitar may seem daunting at first, we have developed a cirruculum to teach our students quickly and effectively.
